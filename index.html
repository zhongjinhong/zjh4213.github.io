Research Interests

My research mainly focuses on Computational Intelligence and its Applications. As one of the main complementary techniques, Evolutionary Computation (EC) is widely acknowledged as a family of powerful tools for non-differentiable complex optimization problems that mathematical methods or gradient based approaches are ill-equiped to deal with. Currently, I am particularly intersted in EA-based approaches on:

  • Multi-modal Optimization
  • Dynamic Optimization
  • Large-Scale Global Optimization
  • Path Planning for Unmanned Aerial Vehicles
